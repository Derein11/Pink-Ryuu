About Us

Founded on November 4 2023, Pink Ryuu was born by combining the best Indonesian Mobile Legends players who are already well-known in the international gaming community. At the end of 2023, Pink Ryuu began participating in several tournaments held directly by Mobile Legends such as MICC and MCL. And expanding to other games such as the popular FPS game PUBG.
